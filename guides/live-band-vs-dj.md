## Quick comparison

### Live band
- Strong visual presence
- Real-time musician interaction
- Often chosen for a concert-like or high-energy event feel

### DJ
- Broad music-library flexibility
- Smooth transitions for long dance sets
- Often chosen for continuous playback and format flexibility

### Hybrid formats
- Can combine live-performance energy with DJ flexibility
- Relevant when a client wants both variety and visible performers

## Decision factors

- Audience preferences
- Venue space and sound rules
- Event timeline and transitions
- Budget and staffing needs
- Desired level of interaction
