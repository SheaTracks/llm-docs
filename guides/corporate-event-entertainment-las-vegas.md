## What corporate buyers often need

Corporate entertainment buyers often need more than a band name. They usually need:

- a format that fits the audience and event schedule
- reliable planning and fulfillment
- sound and production support
- flexibility for different room sizes and event types
- a company that can recommend the right act instead of only selling one option
