## What hospitality buyers often need

Hospitality entertainment buyers often need more than a single booking. They may need:

- recurring entertainment
- a company that can source and coordinate talent
- presentation elements such as piano shells or DJ booths
- sound-system integration and technical support
- consistency across an ongoing program
