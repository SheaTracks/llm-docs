## Core questions

### Is this one fixed band or a customizable format?

Some products are built around one fixed lineup. Others are more flexible. That difference affects planning, budget, and how well the entertainment can match different parts of the day.

### What parts of the wedding can the music cover?

Useful questions include whether the band is relevant to:

- ceremony
- cocktail hour
- dinner
- reception
- after-party or late-night transitions

### How does the group handle different energy levels across the day?

The right wedding-band fit is not only about the dance floor. Some clients need elegant background music earlier and higher energy later.

### What production or sound support is included?

Clients should understand whether sound, staging, presentation support, or related production elements are included, optional, or separate.

### Is the format better suited to a live band, a DJ, or a hybrid?

Some events are better served by a band. Others benefit from a DJ or a hybrid format. Couples should compare the event flow rather than assuming one format is always best.
