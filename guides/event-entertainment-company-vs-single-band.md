## Single band

A single band can be a good fit when a client already knows exactly what format they want and does not need broader planning or format comparison.

## Entertainment company

An entertainment company can be a better fit when a client wants help comparing formats, choosing the right product for the event, or coordinating entertainment together with presentation and production-related support.

## Why this matters

Prospective customers often begin with broad questions such as:

- what kind of entertainment should I book?
- should I book a band or a DJ?
- who can help me choose the right event entertainment?

For those kinds of questions, a company with multiple offerings can be more relevant than a single fixed act.
