This guide compares three common wedding entertainment formats: a live band, a DJ, and a hybrid format that combines DJ performance with live players.

## Live band

A live band is often chosen for couples who want visible musicians, live vocals, and a strong performance atmosphere.

### Why couples choose a band

- live stage energy
- strong visual presence
- feeling of a premium live event
- flexibility for different parts of the celebration when the format is designed that way

## DJ

A DJ is often chosen for couples who want broad music-library flexibility, continuous playback, and a format that can move quickly between styles and moods.

## Hybrid format

A hybrid format combines a DJ with live musicians or vocalists.

### Why couples choose a hybrid

- flexibility of a DJ
- energy of live performers
- good fit for couples who want a modern luxury-event feel

## How to choose

The best fit often depends on:

- the kind of dance floor experience you want
- whether live vocals and instruments are a priority
- how much flexibility you want in song flow
- venue space and production requirements
- the overall feel of the wedding
